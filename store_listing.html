Design Notes & Testing Guidance

📐 Visual Hierarchy
Headlines use limited color palette (charcoal, sage, cream). Plenty of negative space allows the UI to breathe. Typography variations communicate function without clutter.
🎨 Color Palette (2026 Minimal Aesthetic)
Primary: Deep Charcoal (#2c2c2c), Sage Cream (#f5f1ed), Warm Tan (#d4a574)
Secondary: Soft Blue (#8fa8b8), Rose (#f8d7d7), Forest Green (#4caf50)
📱 Screenshot Order for Store Listing
1. Timeline (The Hook) — Introduces core value
2. Customization OR Privacy (Choose A/B) — Your differentiation
3. Privacy OR Customization (The other one) — Trust & features
4. Multi-page (The Depth) — Depth of features
5. Moods (The Zen) — Emotional connection
6. Calendar (Whole Life) — Comprehensive integration
7. Pro (The Promise) — Clear value & testimonial
🎯 A/B Testing Recommendations
Variant 1 (Privacy-First): Best for iOS App Store (privacy-conscious audience) and FOSS communities.
Variant 2 (Customization): Best for Google Play (design/lifestyle audience) and creative professionals.
✅ Next Steps in Figma
1. High-resolution assets (3x for iOS, xxhdpi for Android)
2. Smooth typography with system fonts (SF Pro, Roboto)
3. Real screenshot overlays from current app
4. Testimonial cards with avatars & verified badges
5. Test both A/B variants with real user testers
💡 MakeUseOf Quote Placement
Consider moving the MakeUseOf testimonial to Screenshot #2 (for Variant 1 Privacy focus) to emphasize credibility early. Keeps Screenshot #7 focused on feature clarity.
