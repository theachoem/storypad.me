# Support StoryPad with Add-ons 💛

StoryPad's add-ons are the **only way the app earns money** - and they're designed to make your journaling experience even better. When you purchase an add-on, you're not just getting a great feature, you're helping keep StoryPad **simple, open-source, and ad-free** for everyone.

## Why Add-ons Matter

Your support through add-ons helps us:
- Keep StoryPad **completely free** for core features
- Maintain **no ads or tracking** in the app
- Continue **open-source development**
- Add new features and improvements
- Keep our servers running for syncing and updates

Every add-on is a **lifetime purchase** - buy once, keep forever. No subscriptions, no recurring fees.
