### Step 1: Export Data from Spooky

First, you need to generate a compatibility file from the Spooky app.

1.  Open the **Spooky** app.
2.  Open the side menu and navigate to **Backups** \> **Import / Export**.
3.  Select **Export StoryPad JSON (.json)** and tap **Export**.
4.  Save the file to a known location on your device (e.g., your Downloads folder).

> **Note:** This file contains your text, dates, and metadata in a format specifically optimized for StoryPad.

### Step 2: Import Data into StoryPad

1. Download StoryPad if haven't:\
   [![Play Store](https://img.shields.io/badge/Google_Play-414141?style=for-the-badge&logo=google-play&logoColor=white)](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.tc.writestory)
2. Open the **StoryPad** app.
3. Open the side menu and navigate to **Backups** \> **Import / Export**.
4. Tap **Import StoryPad JSON (.json)**.
5. Browse and select the backup file you just exported from Spooky.
6. In the top-right corner, tap **Restore** to begin the migration.
7. **Stay on the screen** until the process completes.

Once finished, your timeline will automatically refresh with your imported entries.
