Introducing Voice Journal add-on! 🎙️📝

Exciting update! We're launching our brand new Voice Journal add-on, allowing you to capture your thoughts through voice recordings and have them automatically transcribed into your journal entries.

**Key Features:**

- 🎙️ **Voice Recording**: Easily record your thoughts and memories
- 📝 **Automatic Transcription**: Your voice is transcribed into written entries
- ✨ **Seamless Integration**: Works with your existing journal workflow
- 🔒 **Private & Secure**: All recordings stay on your device

Perfect for times when writing feels cumbersome or you prefer speaking your thoughts!

For implementation details, see: [overview.md](https://github.com/theachoem/storypad/blob/2.20.0/docs/features/voice-journal/overview.md)
