This release brings exciting enhancements to your journaling workflow! ✨

We've focused on improving your daily writing experience with better privacy, more editing flexibility, and smarter content management. These updates make StoryPad more secure and enjoyable to use every day.

**Key Improvements:**

- 🔒 **Enhanced Privacy**: Added screen lock feature to keep your journal secure
- ✏️ **Better Editing**: Improved multi-editing capabilities across the app
- 🎨 **Refined Experience**: Better home page navigation and content management
- 🛠️ **Behind the Scenes**: Performance improvements and bug fixes
