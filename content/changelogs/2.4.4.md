## What's New?

Enhanced organization and search capabilities for your journal! ⭐🔍

This update introduces new ways to organize and find your most important stories. Now you can highlight your favorite memories and quickly search through them when you need to find that special moment.

**Key Features:**
- ⭐ **Favorite Stories**: Mark stories as favorites and customize the favorite icon to match your style
- 🔍 **Search by Stars**: Quickly find your favorite stories with the new star-based search filter
- 📚 **Documentation Site**: Added comprehensive documentation to help you get the most out of StoryPad

Perfect for when you want to quickly revisit your most cherished memories or important journal entries!
