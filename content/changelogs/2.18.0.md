Period Calendar Add-on - track your cycle with ease! 🗓️💜

Introducing our new Period Calendar feature to help you track your cycle, moods, and reflections all in one place.

**Key Features:**

- 📅 **Period Tracking**: Easily track your menstrual cycle with a visual calendar
- 😊 **Mood Tracking**: Log how you're feeling throughout your cycle
- 📝 **Cycle Reflections**: View your journal entries right on your period calendar
- 📊 **Pattern Recognition**: See your cycles at a glance and recognize patterns
- 🏠 **Home Page Widget**: Quick access to your period information from your dashboard

This is a lifetime add-on - buy once and use forever. Perfect for users who want integrated health and wellness tracking!
