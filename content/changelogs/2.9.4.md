## What's New?

Small but meaningful improvements to enhance your daily experience! 🚀

This update focuses on usability enhancements and expanding our international support, making StoryPad more accessible and easier to navigate.

- 🇯🇵 **Enhanced Japanese Support**: Updated Japanese translation thanks to our amazing community contributor @iljinjung

These small touches make a big difference in your daily journaling routine!
