Enhanced navigation and better image management! 📱✨

This update brings improvements to navigation, image handling, and overall app responsiveness.

**Key Features:**

- 🎨 **Full Screen End Drawer**: New full-screen navigation panel for easier access to features
- 🌈 **Dynamic Color Theming**: Beautiful dynamic color system adapts to your device settings
- 📸 **Image Information Display**: View detailed information about your photos (size, date, resolution)
- 👉 **Improved Gesture Controls**: Better back gesture experience for more intuitive navigation

Perfect for users who want a more polished and intuitive navigation experience!
