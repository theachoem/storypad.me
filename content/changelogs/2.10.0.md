## What's New?

Exciting new features to enhance your journaling experience! 📅✨

This release brings one of our most requested features - the **Calendar View** - plus improvements to make navigation even more intuitive. We're also preparing some amazing features that will be coming soon!

**New Features:**
- 📅 **Calendar View**: See your journaling journey at a glance! Navigate through your memories by date and get a beautiful overview of your writing timeline
- 🧭 **Improved Navigation**: Made the home page easier to navigate so you can find what you're looking for faster

**Coming Soon:**
- 🎵 **Relaxing Sounds**: Background music and sounds to set the perfect mood for writing and reading your journal entries

**Full Changelog**: https://github.com/theachoem/storypad/releases/tag/2.10.0
