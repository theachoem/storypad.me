Hey everyone! StoryPad 2.32.0 is now live.

After focusing on the engine in our last update, this release brings some of your most requested features to life, along with a cleaner, sleeker UI. Here is what’s new:

1. **Stats View:**
   - See your writing patterns, top moments, and trends by month or year.
2. **Smart Reminders:**
   - You can now set reminders to write every day, receive "throwback" reminders, or get period reminders (if you have the period calendar add-on enabled).
   - Need something specific? You can easily add custom reminders, too! This feature is completely free.
3. **Tag People:**
   - You can finally tag friends and family per entry (and yes, the "people story count" bug is fixed!).
4. **Customizable App Shortcuts:**
   - Jump directly into your favorite actions from your home screen using our new app shortcuts.
5. **Customize Day Colors:**
   - You can now change the previously pre-defined colors for each day to perfectly match your personal style.
6. **Redesigned Settings & UI:**
   - We decluttered the Settings page for a cleaner experience and tweaked the home screen to display larger images.
7. **Easier Media Sharing:**
   - We added a new share button directly to photos and audio for quick exports.
8. **Free Relax Sounds:**
   - Relax Sounds are now FREE!
   - We've officially removed the paywall so you can use all ambient sounds completely free of charge.
9. **Bug Fixes & Improvements:**
   - Improved Map Journal with smarter location tracking.
   - Faster app loading times and better background auto-syncing.
   - Fixed a critical out-of-memory crash during media exports.

Update to the latest version to try it out! Let us know what you think. 🙌
