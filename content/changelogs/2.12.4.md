MacOS support and smarter backups! 🖥️💾

This release brings StoryPad to more platforms while making your data backups more efficient than ever. We're expanding where you can use StoryPad and optimizing how your precious memories are stored.

**Key Features:**
- 🖥️ **MacOS Support**: StoryPad now works on Mac! Journal across all your Apple devices seamlessly
- 🏘️ **Enhanced Community**: Improved community page for better connection with fellow StoryPad users
- 📦 **Smart Backup Compression**: Revolutionary backup optimization that reduces file sizes by ~85%!

**Backup Compression Results:**
- ✅ **400 stories tested**: From 749.8 KB → 112.2 KB (85% size reduction!)
- Faster uploads, less storage space, same complete data protection

Perfect for users with extensive journals - your backups are now lightning fast! ⚡
