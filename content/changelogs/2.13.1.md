Powerful new customization features to make your journal truly yours! 🎨✨

This release focuses on giving you more creative control over your stories. Whether you want to adjust how your images look, change the writing experience, or personalize the visual style - we've got you covered!

**Key Features:**

- 🖼️ **Enhanced Image Control**: Resize and align images exactly how you want them in your stories
- 📄 **Export Your Stories**: Export individual stories as text or markdown files - perfect for sharing or backing up specific entries
- 🎨 **Story Backgrounds**: Add different color tones to story backgrounds to match your mood or organize by theme
- 📝 **Customizable Font Size**: Adjust text size for the perfect reading and writing experience
- 🎛️ **Streamlined Interface**: Moved some actions to a clean sheet view to reduce clutter and improve focus

Your journal, your way! These updates give you the creative freedom to make each story uniquely yours.
