## What's New?

Important stability fixes to keep your journaling experience smooth! 🛠️✨

This focused update addresses several technical issues discovered after the 2.16.0 release, ensuring StoryPad runs reliably on all devices and platforms.

**Key Fixes:**
- 🔧 **Enhanced Stability**: Fixed connection issues and improved app reliability
- 🛡️ **Better Error Handling**: Improved backup system with better error recovery
- 🎨 **Visual Fixes**: Resolved calendar text wrapping and home button color issues
- 📱 **Platform Support**: Better iOS integration with StoreKit improvements

These behind-the-scenes improvements ensure your daily journaling experience remains uninterrupted and enjoyable. Sometimes the best updates are the ones you don't notice - they just make everything work better!
