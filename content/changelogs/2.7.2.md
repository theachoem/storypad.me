## What's New?

Building on our 2.7.0 foundation with important fixes and enhancements! 🛠️

This update focuses on making StoryPad more reliable and user-friendly. We've listened to your feedback and fixed several issues while adding new capabilities to make your journaling experience even better.

**Key Improvements:**
- 💾 **Reliable Draft Saving**: Fixed those annoying draft saving glitches - your work is now safely preserved
- 🔐 **Enhanced Security**: Better protection for your sensitive data with secure storage
- 📝 **Improved Editor**: Better context menu and smoother editing experience
- 🎨 **Visual Refinements**: Improved dark/light mode consistency and better content preview on home
- 🔍 **Better Organization**: New filter button and enhanced multi-editing on search & tag pages
- 📤 **Easy Sharing**: New share app button to spread the word about StoryPad
