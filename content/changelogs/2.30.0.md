Hey everyone! The latest update is now live. Here is a quick look at what’s new:

- 📍 **Map Journal:** You can now add locations to your stories and visualize your memories on a map!
- 💾 **Backup & Restore:** Added a dedicated section in Settings to easily manage your data backups.
- 🏷️ **Yearly Tag View:** Entries in the Tag page are now organized by year for better browsing.
- 📅 **Calendar Shortcut:** Simply tap on the date from the entry page to instantly jump to the calendar view.
- **Week Customization:** You can now choose your preferred "First Day of the Week" in Settings.
- 🔗 **Link Handling:** When clicking a link, you now have the option to either copy or open it.

Check it out and let us know what you think!
