## What's Changed

### ✨ Features
* **Introducing Map Journal** 🗺️ by @theachoem in #643, #644, #645
  Visualize your memories geographically. We've added a new map picker UI and a dedicated database core to support location-based journaling and polishing.
* **Decoupled from Firebase** ☁️ by @theachoem in #641
  Improved privacy and independence by removing hard dependencies on Firebase/Cloud services.
* **Initial Linux support** 🐧 by @theachoem in #642
  Storypad is coming to the desktop! This marks the initial foundation for our Linux project.
* **New Backup & Restore settings** 💾 by @theachoem in #649
  You can now find a dedicated backup and restore section within the settings to keep your data safe.
* **Improved Tag View organization** 🏷️ by @theachoem in #652
  Finding old memories is now easier; entries in the "Tag" page are now split and organized by year.
* **Customizable first day of the week** 📅 by @theachoem in #650
  Whether you start your week on Sunday or Monday, you can now set your preference in the settings.
* **Enhanced Link & Calendar interactions** by @theachoem
  * When clicking a link, you can now choose to either **copy or open** it (#651).
  * Tap on an entry date to **instantly open the calendar** view (#653).
