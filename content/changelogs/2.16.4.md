Personalization meets convenience! 🎨📱

This exciting update introduces powerful customization options while making StoryPad more accessible and enjoyable for daily journaling.

**Key Features:**
- 🔤 **Global Font Size Control**: Customize text size throughout the entire app to match your reading preferences
- ⏰ **Time Display by Default**: Timestamps now show by default to help you track when memories were captured
- 📄 **Improved Page Layouts**: Better organization and visual flow for easier reading and writing
- 🌐 **Enhanced Cloud Integration**: Improved Google Drive connectivity for seamless backup management
- 🎵 **Offline Relax Sounds**: Download calming sounds to play offline while journaling
- 📚 **Template Archives**: Better organization of your custom writing templates
- 🔗 **Community Connection**: New Reddit integration to connect with fellow StoryPad users

Perfect for users who want a more personalized and connected journaling experience!
