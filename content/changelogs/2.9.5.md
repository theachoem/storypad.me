## What's New?

Major iOS improvements and performance optimizations! 🍎⚡

This release brings StoryPad closer to an iOS launch with significant performance enhancements that benefit all users. We've focused on making the app faster, more reliable, and smoother than ever.

**Key Improvements:**
- 🍎 **iOS Support**: Refined the app for iOS release - bringing StoryPad to more platforms!
- 🐛 **Bug Fixes**: Fixed the feeling save issue that some users experienced
- ⚡ **Performance Boost**: 
  - Optimized design system and icons for faster loading
  - Added performance improvements throughout the app
  - Enhanced database operations for smoother experience
  - Better memory management with optimized object handling

The result? A noticeably faster and more responsive journaling experience! 🪴
