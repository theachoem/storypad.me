## What's Changed
1. Storage Management: A new page to help you see and manage how much space your data uses.
2. New Media Options: Support for importing media archives
3. Image/file compression is now enabled by default to keep your journal lightweight.
4. The Map Journal now remembers your preferred map style for a more consistent experience.
5. Bug Fixes & Improvements
