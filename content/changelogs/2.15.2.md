Fine-tuning your personalized journaling experience! 🎵✨

Building on our lifetime add-ons introduced in 2.15.0, this update brings refinements to make your writing environment even more customizable and enjoyable.

**Key Improvements:**

- 📝 **Expanded Title Options**: Increase title font size for better visibility and emphasis
- 🎵 **Enhanced Relaxing Sounds**: New mix feature lets you blend different ambient sounds to create your perfect writing atmosphere
- ☁️ **Improved Backup**: Better Google Drive integration that properly handles deleted records for more reliable syncing

These updates ensure your personalized StoryPad experience works exactly how you want it to, whether you're setting the mood with relaxing sounds or organizing your thoughts with better visual hierarchy.
