## Highlights ✨

<img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/31205268-773c-4c90-aebe-bdc896aaa862" alt="v2.14.0" width="600">

🕓 Throwback is one of the most meaningful features in a journaling app. It gently surfaces memories you captured on this day - 1, 2, or even 3 years ago, letting you reflect on how far you’ve come.

Now you can revisit the past, reflect in the present, and continue your story - right from your timeline.
