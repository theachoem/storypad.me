## What's New?

Beyond the amazing Throwback feature, this release brings thoughtful improvements to make StoryPad more accessible and personalized for everyone:

**Key Features:**
- 🕰️ **Throwback Feature**: Discover memories from previous years on the same date - your personal time machine!
- ⏰ **Time Format Choice**: Switch between 12-hour and 24-hour time formats to match your preference
- ♿ **Accessibility**: Enhanced screen reader support for better accessibility
- 🎯 **Cleaner Interface**: Moved rating and sharing options to a dedicated drawer for less clutter

[Full Changelog](https://github.com/theachoem/storypad/compare/2.13.1...2.14.0)
