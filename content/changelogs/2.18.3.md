## What's New?

Better template management and redemption improvements! 🎁✨

This update focuses on making premium templates more discoverable and streamlining the redemption process.

**Key Features:**

- 👁️ **Smart Template Display**: Templates are now hidden until purchased, reducing unnecessary pressure to buy
- 🎫 **Native Redemption Flow**: Using Apple's native redeem code interface for better security
- 💳 **Improved IAP Experience**: Streamlined in-app purchase flow

Perfect for users who prefer a cleaner, less aggressive shopping experience!
