## What's New?

Capturing memories just got even easier! 📸✨

This update focuses on streamlining your photo-journaling experience with faster access to camera functionality and improved international support.

**Key Features:**
- 📷 **Quick Camera Access**: Camera button now available directly on the home page - capture moments instantly without navigating through menus
- 🌏 **Better Chinese Support**: Improved Chinese (Simplified) localization for our international users
- 🔄 **Enhanced Syncing**: Simplified sync process for better reliability
- 🛠️ **Behind the Scenes**: Various dependency updates and performance improvements

Perfect for those spontaneous moments when you want to quickly capture a memory with both photos and words!
