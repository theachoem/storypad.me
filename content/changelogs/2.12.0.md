## Summary

Mostly just bug fixes, but the most important change is adding a tag tab to the calendar view. It’s a small change but a meaningful one.

Use cases:

- How often did I write about my project (e.g. StoryPad)?
- How many days did I work out? 💪 + track progress
- Tracking my sleep to get up earlier 😴
- Journaling moods, habits, or goals at a glance
- And more...
