Backup improvements and community support! 🤝💾

This update focuses on making your backups more reliable and adding support for our growing international community.

**Key Features:**

- 🇷🇺 **Russian Language Support**: Now available for Russian-speaking users (thanks @ZloyWolf!)
- 💾 **Enhanced Backup Handling**: Better error management and recovery for cloud backups
- 📅 **Event Database Backup**: Your event data now backs up to the cloud automatically
- 📚 **Improved Documentation**: Better guides for period tracking features
