Enhanced security and better sync transparency! 🔐📊

Building on our calendar feature from 2.12.0, this update focuses on protecting your private content and giving you clearer insight into your data synchronization.

**Key Features:**
- 🔒 **Archive Security**: Added security requirements to access archived stories and deleted items - keeping your private content truly private
- 📊 **Sync Status Clarity**: Get detailed information about your syncing status so you always know when your data is safely backed up
- 📅 **Calendar Toggle**: Enhanced calendar view with the ability to toggle date display for cleaner viewing
