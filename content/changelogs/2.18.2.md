## What's New?

Expanded relax sounds and improved performance! 🎵✨

This update brings more calming sounds for your journaling experience and better app performance.

**Key Features:**

- 🎵 **More Music Options**: Added 3 new relaxing music tracks (piano + guitar)
- 🎵 **Piano & Guitar Collections**: Enjoy diverse musical moods while journaling
- 💾 **Automatic Cleanup**: Old downloaded files are automatically cleaned up to free space
- 📱 **Improved Pages Feature**: Fixed issues with the pages button during story creation
- 🧪 **A/B Testing Improvements**: Testing different unlock messages to enhance user experience

Perfect for users who want an enhanced audio experience for their journaling!
