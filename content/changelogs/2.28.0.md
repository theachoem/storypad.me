## What’s new!

1. Insert images as albums 🖼️
Heads-up: To keep things balanced for Pro users, all users can create 2-photo inline albums. Pro unlocks unlimited photos with additional album layouts.

2. Custom home list style (Advanced) 🎨
   *Personalize how your stories appear on the home screen.*
   *It’s free and available under advanced settings.*

3. Default story preferences (free) ⚙️
   *Tired of selecting background and layout every time you create a new journal? Set your default preferences once, and they’ll be applied automatically to all new stories.*

4. Improvements & Fixes
   * Faster loading for yearly archives
   * Improved backup sync performance
   * Fixed bulk actions only reloading the last entry
   * Archives & bin now separated by year tabs
   * App remembers your last opened template tab
   * UI improvements:
     * Time is now shown at the top
     * Preferences save button moved to the app bar
     * Improved background/demo images
   * Updated wording (bookmark instead of star)
   * Various bug fixes and performance improvements
