## What’s new!

1. You can now purchase without connecting to Google Drive.\
   *You can still connect it optionally if you want to sync your purchase across platforms (e.g. iOS).*
2. Period Calendar is now free 🌸\
   *It’s turned off by default; enable it from the sidebar under Add-Ons.*
3. Flexible Add-Ons
   * Add-ons can now be turned on or off anytime.
   * Don’t like Relax Sounds? Simply switch it off.
4. We’ve also simplified our pricing.\
   We’ve made pricing easier with a one-time purchase: **StoryPad Pro (9.99$) 🎖️**
   * All previous add-ons are now included
   * Already bought add-ons? You’ll get StoryPad Pro automatically at no extra cost
   * All future updates are included; no additional purchases needed
5. Improvements & Fixes
   * Added Turkish language support 🇹🇷
   * Fixed auto-sync issues with backups
   * Removed pin limit; you can now pin unlimited notes
   * Various bug fixes and performance improvements
